The Louvre Pyramids

The Louvre Pyramids is one of The Louvre's biggest attraction along with France's well known painting the Mona Lisa made by an equally famous renaissance man Leonardo Da Vinci along with over five-thousand other paintings. The main pyramid is accompanied by two smaller twin pyramids on either side of the larger pyramid.
